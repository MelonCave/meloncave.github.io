## Transformative Discipleship Technology (TDT)

TDT focuses on using available resources to improve cognitive awareness by applying neuroscience principles and developing Christian mindfulness disciplines. It integrates approaches from neurohacking, meta-cognition skill development, becoming a more proficient human-in-the-loop (HITL) for machine learning systems, understanding the algorithms of large language models (LLMs), and utilizing AI as cognitive-support technology.

In essence, TDT is about leveraging the neuroplastic brain given to us by our Creator to *program ourselves* toward eliminating detrimental habits and improving our discipline as Christ's disciples. One of our key mottos is **"Discipline Equals Freedom!"** Although we are not satisfied with our current level of discipline, we remain extremely grateful for the freedoms and technological blessings we enjoy. We apply these blessings and freedoms to help us **transform our abilities as disciples!**

TDT functions primarily as a meta-objectiveâ€”a form of Christian cognitive neuroscience application or neurohacking. The purpose of TDT is to work on habits and improve our ability to achieve other spiritual objectives through meta-cognition, understanding learning models, and using AI to better program ourselves away from negative patterns. Rather than being solely an objective, ***TDT is certainly NOT an end in and of itself for us.***
