We looked at other options and basically decided that our best choices probably was Strawberry Browser OR BrowserOS

Based on 2025 reviews and official details, Strawberry Browser and BrowserOS are both agentic AI browsers designed for task automation, but they cater to different needs. Strawberry focuses on specialized, collaborative AI companions for professional workflows, while BrowserOS emphasizes open-source, privacy-focused, on-device automation. Neither fully replaces traditional browsers like Chrome yet, but they excel in multi-agent handling of web tasks. Here's a detailed side-by-side comparison:

### Key Differences & Recommendations
- **Automation Approach**: Strawberry uses role-specific companions for collaborative, approved workflows, ideal for business efficiency. BrowserOS leverages natural language for on-device agents, prioritizing privacy and simplicity without ongoing costs.
- **Maturity & Focus**: Strawberry is more polished for pro use but subscription-based; BrowserOS is lightweight and community-driven, better for tinkerers.
