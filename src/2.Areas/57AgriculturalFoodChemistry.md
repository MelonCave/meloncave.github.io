# Agriculture and Food Chemistry

[Agriculture and Food Chemistry](https://chemrxiv.org/engage/chemrxiv/category-dashboard/605c72ef153207001f6470dd) focuses on the chemical processes involved in food production, preservation, and nutrition. It explores the composition of agricultural products, pesticides, and fertilizers to enhance crop yield and safety. Research often addresses food quality, contaminants, and sustainable farming practices through chemical analysis.
